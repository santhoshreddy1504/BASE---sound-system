EXPERIENCE IN STORE

CASE STUDY: KEF AUDIO HOME
CINEMA SYSTEM

Senso Systems designed and installed a bespoke media wall tailored to the client's needs. This wall elegantly housed the left, right, and center channel speakers while providing a sleek mount for the TV. It served as both a functional and aesthetic centerpiece, ensuring all equipment was seamlessly integrated. The audio-visual upgrade

featured an Arcam AVR processor, delivering impactful and authoritative sound performance. A Sonance ISW in-ceiling subwoofer provided deep bass without visible clutter, while Sonance IS4 in-ceiling Dolby Atmos speakers created a multidimensional sound field, ensuring a fully cinematic experience.

The final installation struck the perfect balance between performance and design. The media wall kept all components and cabling hidden, maintaining a clean, minimalist look. The sound system exceeded the client's expectations, delivering rich, engaging audio while remaining discrete and stylish.
