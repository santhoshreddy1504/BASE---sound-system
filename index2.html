SO WHAT ARE WE
TALKING ABOUT?

Hi-fi, short for higher fidelity, encompasses stereo equipment designed for music playback across various mediums, such as such as vinyl records, CDs, digital streaming, and electronic applications like preamplifiers.

Creating a superior system requires nuanced understanding and experience. Trusting both ears and expertise is key to our approach. Factors like positioning, source, cables, isolation, and power conditioning are crucial for an audio experience that truly moves you.

EXPERIENCE IN STORE

KII HOME SETUP

Senso Systems conducted an on-site visit to analyse the customer’s room frequency response and its impact on their Kii Three speakers’ performance. Using advanced tools, we adjusted the Kii speakers’ built-in EQ software to align with the acoustic characteristics of the environment. This ensured the speakers operated in harmony with the room’s layout and materials, eliminating distortions and inconsistencies.

The tuning process resulted in a significantly more balanced and detailed sound presentation. The client experienced an elevated listening experience, with enhanced clarity and precision that maximised the potential of their Kii Three speakers. This project highlighted our commitment to delivering bespoke acoustic solutions and after-sales service.
